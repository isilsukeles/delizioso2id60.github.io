BBQ Chicken Cornbread Skillet

  1. Home
  2. Dinner
  3. BBQ Chicken Cornbread Skillet
Total Time: 0:50        Cook: 0:10        Level: Easy        Serves: 6

You had us at ... well, all of the above.

BBQ Chicken Cornbread Pie is dangerously addicting! You will be making this over and over again.

    Ingredients

  • 1 box JIFFY corn muffin mix
  • 1/2 c. sour cream
  • 2 tbsp. butter, melted
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 c. corn kernels (frozen, canned, or fresh)
  • 1 red onion, chopped
  • kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 c. rotisserie chicken, shredded
  • 1 1/3 c. BBQ sauce, divided
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 1 c. shredded Cheddar
  • 1 c. shredded gouda
  • 1/4 c. chopped green onions

    Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°.
  2. Prepare cornbread base: In a medium bowl, mix together corn muffin mix, sour cream, butter, egg and corn. Pour into a large cast iron skillet and bake until golden brown on top, 20 minutes. Let cool slightly in pan.
  3. Meanwhile, make the barbecue filling: Cook onions over medium heat until soft, then season with salt and pepper. Add the chicken and stir to break up meat. Stir in 1 cup BBQ sauce and lime juice, then season with salt and pepper and cook until warmed through.
  4. Poke entire surface of cooled cornbread with a fork, then pour remaining 1/3 cup BBQ sauce over the cornbread. Add the meat mixture and top with shredded cheeses. Cover with foil and bake until the cheese is melted, about 20 minutes. Remove foil and broil until cheese begins to brown, about 5 minutes.
  5. Top with green onions and serve.
