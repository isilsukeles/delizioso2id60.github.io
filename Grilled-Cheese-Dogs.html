Grilled Cheese Dogs

Satisfy your ultimate lunchtime cravings with this easy recipe for the best grilled cheese hot dogs. Grilled Cheese Hot Dogs are a super fun and tasty combination of 2 classics- Grilled Cheese and Hot Dogs. A buttery, crispy hot dog bun encases a grilled hot dog with lots of melty cheese and crispy bacon. unch doesnâ€™t get any better than this!

Ingredients

  • 4 hot dog buns
  • 2 tbsp. butter, softened
  • 1/4 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp. onion powder
  • 4 hot dogs, sliced lengthwise
  • 3 c. shredded Cheddar
  • Sliced green onions, for garnish

Directions

  1. Flatten hot dog buns with a rolling pin. In a small bowl, stir together butter, garlic powder, and onion powder. Spread all over bottom side of buns.
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat, sear hot dogs (working in batches if necessary) until charred, 2 minutes per side. Set aside.
  3. Place bun buttered side down in skillet and top with 1/2 cup cheddar. Top with a hot dog and sprinkle with a little more cheddar and green onions.
  4. Repeat with remaining buns and dogs.
