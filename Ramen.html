Beef Ramen Stir Fry

  1. Home
  2. Dinner
  3. Beef Ramen Stir Fry
Total Time: 0:20        Level: Easy        Serves: 4 Servings

Ramen=your new favorite stir fry noodles.

30 Minute Beef and Broccoli Stir Fry Ramen is an easy weeknight dinner. Make it for your family tonight!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp. vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 head broccoli, cut into florets
  • 1 large carrot, cut into half moons
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1/4 c. soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp. brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp. apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tsp. sriracha
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 packets ramen noodles, seasoning packets discarded
  • Sliced green onions, for garnish

Directions

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, heat oil. Add onions, peppers, and broccoli and cook until tender, 10 minutes. Add ground beef and cook until no longer pink, 5 minutes more. Drain fat.
  2. Make sauce: Whisk together soy sauce, brown sugar, apple cider vinegar, Sriracha, and garlic.
  3. In a medium saucepan, bring water to a boil. Add cooked ramen noodles and cook until tender, 2 minutes. Drain.
  4. Add cooked ramen noodles to skillet and pour over sauce. Toss until completely combined, then garnish with green onions and serve.
