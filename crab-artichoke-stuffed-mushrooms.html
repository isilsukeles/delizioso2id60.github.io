Crab Artichoke Stuffed Mushrooms

  1. Home
  2. Appetizers & Snacks
  3. Crab Artichoke Stuffed Mushrooms
Total Time: 0:40        Cook: 0:15        Level: Easy        Serves: 8-10

Super delicious crab artichoke stuffed mushrooms

Portobello mushrooms stuffed with artichokes and sun-dried tomatoes and grilled to perfection. A great appetizer for any party!

    Ingredients

  • 20 button mushrooms, cleaned and stems removed
  • 8 oz. cream cheese, softened
  • 1 c. shredded Monterey Jack, divided
  • 1/2 c. freshly grated Parmesan
  • 1 14-oz. can artichoke hearts, drained and finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 12 oz. lump crab meat
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Chopped parsley, for garnish

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°. In a large bowl, combine cream cheese, Monterey Jack, Parmesan, artichoke hearts, garlic, crab, green onions and Worcestershire sauce. Season with salt and pepper and stir until fully combined.
  2. Stuff mushrooms with mixture and transfer to a small baking sheet.
  3. Bake until mushrooms are cooked and mixture is warmed through, 25 minutes. Broil if desired.
  4. Garnish with parsley and serve.
