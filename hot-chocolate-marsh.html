Festive Hot Chocolate

  1. Home
  2. Drinks
  3. Festive Hot Chocolate
Total Time: 0:15        Prep: 0:15        Level: Easy        Serves: 6 Cups

Hot Chocolate you must make this winter !

You can’t beat a steaming hot cup of creamy hot chocolate on a cold winters day. This delicious, mildly spiced version has the option of an extra bit of oomph when you add some brandy or rum. What could be more delicious or warming when coming in from a cold Christmas walk or after spending hours on your feet Christmas shopping?

Ingredients

  • 2 c. whole milk
  • 2 tbsp. cocoa powder
  • 30g dark chocolate, finely grated
  • ¼tsp ground cinnamon
  • A pinch of grated nutmeg
  • 2tbsp brandy or dark rum (optional)
  • Marshmallows, macarons and brownsugar to serve

Directions

  1. Place the milk in a saucepan and heat gently until almost boiling and scalding hot. Alternatively heat in a microwave on HIGH/100% for 2-3 mins.
  2. Meanwhile mix the drinking chocolate powder, grated chocolate, cinnamon and nutmeg and brandy or rum (if using) in a heatproof jug until well combined.
  3. As soon as the milk is about to boil, pour it into the jug, whisking with a balloon whisk (or stirring with a wooden spoon) as you do.
  4. Immediately pour into cups. Generously scatter Marshmallows to decorate and serve with macarons.
